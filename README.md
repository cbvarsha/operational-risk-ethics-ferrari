# Operational Risk & Ethical Evaluation of Ferrari N.V.
I’ve published an operational risk & ethics analysis of Ferrari N.V. on GitHub, applying Basel II and CFA frameworks with ESG focus.



## Overview
This repository contains an academic operational risk and ethics assessment of Ferrari N.V., conducted using the Basel II Operational Risk Framework and the CFA Institute Code of Ethics.

The project evaluates how a global luxury automotive brand manages cybersecurity, regulatory pressure, supply chain disruptions, ESG risks, and reputational challenges.

## Frameworks Used
- Basel II – Operational Risk Classification
- CFA Institute Code of Ethics and Standards of Professional Conduct
- ESG & Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)

## Key Risk Areas Analyzed
- Cybersecurity & digital systems risk
- Supply chain disruption (COVID-19, semiconductor shortages)
- Regulatory & emissions compliance
- Reputational risk from Formula 1 operations
- Labor practices & workplace safety

## Ethical & ESG Focus
- Integrity and transparency in regulatory investigations
- Fair dealing in customer allocation practices
- Third-party due diligence and supplier screening
- ESG disclosure gaps (Scope 3 emissions, third-party assurance)

## Key Recommendations
- Improve transparency in regulatory settlements
- Enhance cybersecurity preparedness and simulation drills
- Expand ESG disclosure with Scope 3 emissions reporting and third-party audits
